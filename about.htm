XY Feng

I am an artistic coder, designer, and maker who blends technology and art. My work has been exhibited widely at ITP Gallery, Gizmodo Gallery, Sony Wonder Lab, IAC, Wieden+Kennedy, NIME, during and after I was pursuing M.P.S in Interaction Design from ITP, Tisch School of the Arts at New York University. Originating from a small city at the foot of Mount Tai, I am currently based in New York City.
